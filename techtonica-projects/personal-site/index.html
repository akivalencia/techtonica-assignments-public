About Adilene

Adilene is a first-generation student and a recent graduate of Williams College. She is currently a software engineer apprentice at Techtonica. She loves languages (natural and programming alike)! In her free time, she enjoys flower arrangement, interior design, and weight training. Her dream is to become a role model to other children and young adults who come from low-income/BIPOC and immigrant backgrounds. Another of her goals is to learn sign language in order to make more friends!
